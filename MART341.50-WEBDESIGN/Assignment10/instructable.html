Materials Needed

  • Fabric (lightweight cotton)
  • Elastic band (for waistband)
  • Measuring tape
  • Fabric scissors
  • Pins or clips
  • Sewing machine or needle and thread
  • Iron
  • Safety pin

Instructions

  1. Measure and cut your fabric.
  2. Prep the fabric.
  3. Sew the side seam.
  4. Create the waistband casing.
  5. Insert the elastic.
  6. Hem the bottom of the skirt.
  7. Adjust and finish.
  8. Try it on and enjoy!

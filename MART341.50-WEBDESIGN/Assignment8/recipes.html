Sweet Grass Tea

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried sweet grass
  • 2 cups water

Instructions:

  1. Boil the water.
  2. Add sweet grass and let steep for 10 minutes.
  3. Strain and enjoy!

Buffalo Tallow Balm

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup buffalo tallow
  • 10 drops essential oils (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Melt buffalo tallow in a double boiler.
  2. Add essential oils and stir.
  3. Pour into a jar and let cool.
